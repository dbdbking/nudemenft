NudemeNFT



The NudemeNFT is a collection of 9,999 unique interactive artworks living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each artwork (NFT) is made up of 1 to 2,500 programmed Nudemen, relentlessly forming a pattern, an image or a message chosen from hundreds of curated visuals.

On-chain Metadata

When you buy (mint) a NudemeNFT token, a unique set of traits (metadata) will be generated based on a block hash combined with your token ID. These traits will be stored on the Ethereum blockchain immutably. They are like the DNA of your Nudemen, which determines their personality, quantity, formation, and behaviour (such as how fast they move and even how hesitant they are to visualize the art that they represent).

Viewing the Artwork

After minting your token, your artwork will begin to exist on OpenSea. You can view and interact with it in any modern browser that supports Javascript (p5.js). The program to render and display the artwork is hosted permanently on a decentralized file system (IPFS). The image on OpenSea will be updated after the token is minted. Nudemen love bigger spaces, so they are best viewed on larger screens.

How to Buy/Mint

The NudemeNFT can only be minted directly from our smart contract on Etherscan at 0.09ETH. Up to 5 tokens can be minted with each transaction. Minting will be closed permanently after 11/01/2021 11pm EST.

Minting from a contract requires technical understanding of how smart contracts and the Ethereum blockchain work. All transactions on the blockchain are final and completely irreversible. Please only proceed if you know what you are doing.

mintNudemen()
Select mintNudemen under Write Contract. For example if you want to mint 2 tokens, set payableAmount to 0.18, _to to YourWalletAddress and _mintAmount to 2. Make sure you have already connected to your Web3 Wallet and then click Write.

About The Nudemen

The Nudemen Series is an ongoing art project by Francis Lam that aims to raise sociological questions in the post-Internet era. The project was originally inspired and created to counterbalance the overwhelming ubiquity of female nudity throughout the Internet (Nudemessenger, 2003). The series has taken a utopian approach to depict technology, culture and reality in different forms and media.

The Nudemen Series has been awarded and exhibited by Ars Electronica, Japan Media Arts Festival, New Vision Arts Festival, CreativeApplications and others, and was Featured on the Apple App Store .
