Nudemen Checks



Nudemen Checks is an NFT collection of up to 999 unique interactive arts inspired by Jack Butcher's Checks. Each artwork (NFT) is made up of 80 programmed Nudemen, relentlessly forming an art of Checks based on their on-chain metadata. To know more about The Nudemen Project, check out NudemeNFT.com.

How to Mint

Nudemen Checks will first be claimed by NudemeNFT owners for free from our smart contract. Please follow us on Twitter @Nudemenft for news and announcements.
