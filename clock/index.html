Nudemen Clock



The Nudemen Clock is an NFT collection of up to 999 unique interactive clocks living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each is made up of 1 to 1,152 programmed Nudemen, relentlessly forming a digital or analog clock based on their on-chain metadata. To know more about The Nudemen Project, check out NudemeNFT.com.

How to Claim/Mint

The Nudemen Clock can be claimed by NudemeNFT owners for free or minted directly from our smart contract. Claimed Clocks will share the same ID, traits and behaviors as their original NudemeNFTs. Owners can preview the clock before minting by adding "clock/" to the NudemeNFT direct link, which can be found on the top right corner of its OpenSea page (next to the Share Button). You can also use the tokenURI function to check if your clock has been claimed.

2. claimClock()
E.g., if you own NudemeNFT #23, then select claimClock under Write Contract and set nudemeNFT_id to 23. Make sure you have already connected to your Web3 Wallet and then click Write.

New Nudemen Clocks can be minted at 0.05ETH. Up to 2 tokens can be minted with each transaction. Newly minted clocks will feature traits and behaviors generated by the same algorithm as the NudemeNFTs.

3. mintClock()
E.g., if you want to mint 2 clocks, set payableAmount to 0.1 and _mintAmount to 2. Make sure you have already connected to your Web3 Wallet and then click Write.
