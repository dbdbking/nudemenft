Nudemen Clock



The Nudemen Clock is an NFT collection of up to 999 unique interactive clocks living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each is made up of 1 to 1,152 programmed Nudemen, relentlessly forming a digital or analog clock based on their on-chain metadata. To know more about The Nudemen Project, check out NudemeNFT.com.

How to Claim/Mint

Currently, the Nudemen Clock can only be claimed by NudemeNFT owners directly from our smart contract. Claimed Clocks will share the same traits and behaviours as their original NudemeNFTs. Owners can preview the clock before minting by adding "clock/" to the NudemeNFT direct link, which can be found on the top right corner of its OpenSea page (next to the Share Button).

claimClock()
E.g., if you own NudemeNFT #23, then select claimClock under Write Contract and set nudemeNFT_id to 23. Make sure you have already connected to your Web3 Wallet and then click Write.

Any unclaimed clocks will be available for public minting at a later date, and will feature traits and behaviours generated by the same algorithm as the NudemeNFTs.
